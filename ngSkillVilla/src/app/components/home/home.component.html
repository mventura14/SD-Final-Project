About Skill Villa

Skill Villa is our final project in the Skill Distillery Bootcamp. The application aims to create communities for people to learn, improve, and share skills. We believe that the best way to learn is by creating a space for groups of like minded people pursuing the same goals. It was built with a SpringBoot backend and Angular frontend during a 6-day sprint.

Purpose of Skill Villa

The purpose of Skill Villa is to provide a method for creating communities focused on skill development. Users can learn new skills, improve existing ones, and share their knowledge with others.
